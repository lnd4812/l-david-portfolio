Depeche Mode

Who is Depeche Mode?

Depeche Mode is a British trio that first burst onto the music scene in the 70's and which is still in business almost 50 years later.

Members of the Band

For most of their 40+ year history, there have been 3 main members:

  • Martin Gore
  • Andy Fletcher
  • Dave Gahan

Dave Gahan became part of the group early on and it remained a foursome until 1981, when Vince Clarke departed the band, teaming up not long after with Alison Moyet to form Yaz.

It became a foursome again when Dave and the two remaining original members, Martin Gore and Andy Fletcher were joined by Alan Wilder in 1982.

Upon the departure of Alan 13 years later in 1995, Depeche Mode was once again a trio and have remained so to this day.
